Welcome to Remine India!

We are a startup that specializes in the recycling of e-waste and Li batteries. Our mission is to reduce electronic waste and to protect the environment by promoting the responsible disposal of electronic devices and batteries.

At Remine India, we offer a range of recycling services, including:

  • Collection of e-waste and Li batteries
  • Transportation and storage of e-waste and Li batteries
  • Dismantling and sorting of e-waste and Li batteries
  • Recycling and disposal of e-waste and Li batteries

Our team of experts is committed to ensuring that the recycling process is carried out in an environmentally responsible and sustainable manner. Contact us today to learn more about our services!

Our Services

E-Waste Recycling

We offer collection, transportation, and recycling of electronic waste, including computers, laptops, mobile phones, and other electronic devices.

Li Battery Recycling

We offer collection, transportation, and recycling of lithium-ion batteries, which are commonly used in laptops, mobile phones, and other electronic devices.

Data Destruction

We provide secure data destruction services to ensure that your confidential information is not compromised during the recycling process.
